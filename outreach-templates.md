## 5. Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

### Phase 1 — Opening & Rapport (2 minutes)

"[First Name], thanks for making the time. Before we dive in, I want to set a quick agenda so we make the most of our 30 minutes:

1. I'd love to understand your current setup and where things feel painful
2. I'll share a few ideas based on what I've seen work for similar companies
3. If it makes sense, we'll talk about next steps

Sound good? Great — let's start with you."

---

### Phase 2 — Current State Mapping (10 minutes)

**Situation Questions:**
- "Can you walk me through how [specific process] works today, end to end?"
- "How many people are involved in that workflow?"
- "What tools or systems are you currently using?"
- "Roughly how many [units — transactions, tickets, requests] do you process per [week/month]?"

**Problem Questions:**
- "Where does the process break down or slow down most often?"
- "What happens when there's an error — how does your team catch and fix it?"
- "How much time per week would you estimate your team spends on the manual parts of this?"
- "Has this gotten worse as you've grown?"

---

### Phase 3 — Gap Identification & Impact (10 minutes)

**Implication Questions:**
- "When [problem] happens, what's the downstream impact?"
- "How does that affect [customer experience / revenue / team morale]?"
- "If you do nothing and grow 2x next year, what happens to this process?"
- "Have you tried to solve this before? What happened?"

**Need-Payoff Questions:**
- "If you could automate [specific task], what would your team do with that time?"
- "What would it mean for [Company Name] if [process] ran at 10x the speed with zero errors?"
- "If we could cut [metric] by [percentage], would that move the needle for your [quarter/year]?"

---

### Phase 4 — Solution Preview (5 minutes)

"Based on what you've shared, here's what I'm thinking at a high level:

[Describe 2-3 sentence solution approach]

We've done something very similar for [Similar Company] — they saw [specific result] within [timeframe].

The typical project like this runs [timeframe] and falls in the [$X-$Y range], but I'd want to scope it properly before giving you a firm number."

---

### Phase 5 — Next Steps & Close (3 minutes)

"Here's what I'd suggest as a next step:

I'll put together a detailed proposal with exact scope, timeline, and pricing based on what we discussed today. You'll have it by [date — within 48 hours].

A few questions before we wrap:
- Besides yourself, who else would need to be involved in the decision?
- Is there a timeline you're working against?
- Is there a budget range you have in mind, so I can tailor the proposal?

Great — I'll have the proposal to you by [date]. Does it make sense to book a 30-minute follow-up for [2-3 days after proposal delivery] to walk through it together?"

[Book the follow-up before hanging up]
